With the move to GeckoLib5, the decision was made to move GeckoLib's assets into its own directory 
to prevent the recurring issue of asset location clashes. As of 5.0, you will need to move your assets as below:

- Models (geo.json): `/assets/<modid>/geckolib/models/`
- Animations (.animation.json): `/assets/<modid>/geckolib/animations/`

Where `/assets/<modid>/` is your mod's normal assets location
